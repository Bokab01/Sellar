                        🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM CONSISTENCY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✅ ANIMATION STANDARDS:

   DURATION GUIDELINES:
   ├─ Micro-interactions: 100-200ms (button press, icon change)
   ├─ Small movements: 300-400ms (shake, small scale)
   ├─ Medium transitions: 500-600ms (slide, fade, scale)
   └─ Large transitions: 800-1000ms (screen change, complex)

   EASING FUNCTIONS:
   ├─ Linear: Simple movements (progress bars)
   ├─ Timing: Fade in/out, slides
   ├─ Spring: Natural feel (buttons, cards, modals)
   └─ Interpolate: Complex animations (rotation, color)

   PERFORMANCE TARGETS:
   ├─ Native Driver: Always use when possible (transform, opacity)
   ├─ Frame Rate: Maintain 60fps minimum
   ├─ Jank-Free: No dropped frames on low-end devices
   └─ Battery: Efficient animations (no continuous loops unless needed)
